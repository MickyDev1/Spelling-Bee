Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 5th of August 2024

Introduction

Welcome to WordWiz, a spelling bee app designed to enhance your vocabulary and spelling skills. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app and website.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information: When you register for WordWiz, we may collect information such as your name and email address to create your account and personalize your experience.

2. Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with the app, such as the games you play, words you spell, and your scores.

3. Device Information: We may collect data about the device you use to access WordWiz, including the type of device, operating system, and IP address.

How We Use Your Information

1. To Provide and Improve Our Services: We use your information to create and manage your account, provide customer support, and improve the app’s features and functionality.

2. Personalization: Your data helps us tailor your experience by suggesting words or levels based on your performance and preferences.

3. Communication: We may send you notifications about app updates, promotions, and other relevant information.

4. Analytics: We use aggregated and anonymized data to understand how users interact with WordWiz and to improve our services.

Sharing Your Information

1. Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party providers who help us operate the app, such as hosting services, analytics providers, and customer support platforms.

2. Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to legal processes, such as a court order or subpoena.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement reasonable measures to protect it from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

1. Access and Correction: You can access and update your personal information through your account settings.

2. Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the instructions in those communications or by contacting us directly.

3. Account Deletion: You may delete your WordWiz account at any time by contacting our support team.

Children’s Privacy

WordWiz is intended for users of all ages, but we take special care to protect the privacy of children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by email or through the app. Your continued use of WordWiz after the changes take effect indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at Word-Wiz.

We are committed to improving your spelling skills and overall knowledge.
