What is Wordwiz?

Wordwiz is an online spelling bee platform designed to help students of all ages improve their spelling skills and compete with others in a fun and engaging way.

What levels of difficulty are available on Wordwiz?

We offer three levels of difficulty: Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced. Each level features words tailored to your skill level

How do competitions work on Wordwiz?

Competitions are held online, and participants are given a set amount of time to spell a series of words. The participant with the most correct spelling wins

How do I track my progress?

Your dashboard displays your progress, including your spelling accuracy, competition results, and badges earned.
